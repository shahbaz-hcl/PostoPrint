1. How do I order my Photo Prints?

Postalgia Prints is your Smartphone's Personal Photo Printer. Upload your photos seamlessly, and get high quality prints posted straight to your mailbox!

Three Simple Steps:
  1. Choose Photos from your phone's photo album
  2. Customize the Size and Quantity of Photos chosen for Printing
  3. Checkout and Pay with a Credit/Debit Card

It's as simple as that! Go on now, and turn your phone' photos into lasting memories for your family and friends!

3. What is the delivery time?

Your photos should reach you in 3 or less working days.

At Postalgia Prints, we ensure that your order is posted out on the same day to guarantee that you receive your prints in the shortest time possible!

4. What is the Quality of Postalgia Photo Prints

We offer 3 photo print sizes: 4R (6" x 4"), Wallet size prints (3 3/8" x 2 1/8") and Square size prints (3" x 3").

Our 4R photos are printed using Revolutionary Dye-Sublimation Printing Technology, producing greater picture Quality, and longer lasting photos! 4R photos are printed with a glossy finish, without borders.

Our Wallet & Square prints are printed on durable 300gsm paper with a nostalgic touch. Perfect for your trip down memory lane.

** The Quality of your Photo Prints are largely dependent on the Quality of the Photos taken and submitted to us. Follow us on Facebook for more Smartphone Photography Tips to help you take better photos with your Smartphone today!

5. Does Postalgia store my Photos?

Your Privacy is our utmost importance, therefore we do not store photos. You have other Big Boys doing that for you already.

6. Do I have to pay for postage?

For orders more than $10, Postage will be FREE! Otherwise, the Postage Fee is $1.

7. How do I make payment?

At the Checkout page, tap on the "Pay with Paypal" icon to make Payment. You will be directed to the Payment gateway, where you can either login to your Paypal account or pay with a Credit/Debit Card.

8. How do I use the discount code

At the Checkout page, tap on the "Enter Disc. Code" field. Key in the Discount Code, and click "return" to activate.

If an Invalid Discount Code error message pops up, the Discount Code you used may have expired.

9. How do I print the same photo in 2 different sizes

Tap on the "Albums" button to return to the Albums page to choose a new Photo. Select the same photos and add them to your Print Roll again.

10. Does Postalgia do Color Correction for me?

Not at the moment. Please do Color Correction with 3rd party software before sending them in for Printing. We want to be able to deliver what you see on your smartphone's screen.

Follow us on Facebook for Photo editing Tips & App recommendations, and be the Photo editing master you always wanted to be!

11. I ran into a problem with my App/Order/Prints, what should I do?

Please contact us by sending an email with your order number in the subject header to smile@postalgiaprints.com. Hang in there while we get back to you within the next 24 hours!
